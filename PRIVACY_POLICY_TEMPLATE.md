# Privacy Policy for Aqim - Prayer Times App

**Effective Date**: November 2025
**Last Updated**: November 2025
**Privacy Policy URL**: https://megatemran.github.io/aqim/privacy-policy.html

## English Version

### Introduction

Brings2You ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Aqim mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Location Data
- **Type**: Precise location (GPS) and approximate location
- **Purpose**: To calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
- **Collection Method**: Collected when you grant location permission
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device, cached for up to 1 hour
- **Sharing**: Not shared with any third parties

#### 1.2 Camera Access
- **Type**: Camera feed for real-time processing
- **Purpose**: AI-powered prayer rakaat counting using pose detection
- **Processing**: All processing done locally on your device
- **Storage**: Not stored or saved
- **Sharing**: Not shared with any third parties

#### 1.3 Advertising Data
- **Provider**: Google AdMob
- **Data Collected**: Device identifiers, ad interaction data
- **Purpose**: To display relevant advertisements
- **Third Party**: Google LLC
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### 1.4 Device Information
- **Type**: Device model, Android version, app version
- **Purpose**: App functionality and crash reporting
- **Storage**: Local only

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Prayer Time Calculation**: Using your location to fetch accurate prayer times from e-Solat Malaysia or AlAdhan API (International)
- **Notification Services**: Sending prayer time notifications and alarms
- **Qibla Direction**: Calculating the direction to Kaaba from your location
- **App Functionality**: Ensuring widgets display correct prayer times
- **Rakaat Counter**: Processing camera feed for pose detection
- **Advertising**: Displaying relevant ads through Google AdMob

---

### 3. Data Storage and Security

#### Local Storage
- All prayer times are cached locally on your device for 24 hours
- Location data is cached for 1 hour
- User preferences stored in device's local storage
- No server-side storage of personal data

#### Security Measures
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Location data never leaves your device except to fetch prayer times
- Camera feed processed locally, never uploaded
- No user accounts or authentication required

---

### 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we use the following third-party services:

#### 4.1 e-Solat Malaysia API (Malaysia Prayer Times)
- **Website**: https://www.e-solat.gov.my
- **Data Shared**: Location coordinates
- **Purpose**: Fetching prayer times for Malaysian locations
- **Privacy**: Subject to e-Solat's privacy policies

#### 4.2 AlAdhan API (International Prayer Times)
- **Website**: https://aladhan.com
- **Data Shared**: Location coordinates, timestamp
- **Purpose**: Fetching prayer times for international locations
- **Privacy**: Subject to AlAdhan's privacy policies

#### 4.3 Google AdMob
- **Provider**: Google LLC
- **Data Shared**: Advertising ID, device info, ad interaction
- **Purpose**: Displaying advertisements
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Control**: You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings

---

### 5. User Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

#### 5.1 Location Permission
- **Deny Permission**: You can deny location access; app will use Kuala Lumpur as default
- **Revoke Permission**: You can revoke location access anytime in device settings
- **Impact**: Prayer times may not be accurate for your actual location

#### 5.2 Camera Permission
- **Deny Permission**: Rakaat counter feature will not work
- **No Impact**: Other app features remain functional

#### 5.3 Notification Permission
- **Control**: Enable/disable prayer notifications in app settings
- **Individual Control**: Enable/disable notifications for each prayer time separately

#### 5.4 Data Deletion
- **Method**: Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data
- **Result**: All cached prayer times, settings, and preferences will be removed

---

### 6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

### 7. International Users

If you are using the App from outside Malaysia:
- Prayer times will be fetched from AlAdhan API
- Location data is still processed the same way
- Advertising may be subject to your local regulations
- All privacy protections still apply

---

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notification (for major changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

---

### 9. Permissions Explanation

The App requires the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|------------|---------|----------|
| `INTERNET` | Fetch prayer times, display ads | Yes |
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` | Calculate prayer times | Optional |
| `CAMERA` | Rakaat counter feature | Optional |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Prayer time notifications | Optional |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | Exact prayer time alarms | Yes |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Reschedule alarms after restart | Yes |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Wake device for prayer alarms | Yes |
| `VIBRATE` | Vibrate for prayer notifications | Optional |
| `USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT` | Full-screen prayer alarm | Optional |

- Tiada penyimpanan data peribadi di server

- Semua komunikasi API menggunakan enkripsi HTTPS
