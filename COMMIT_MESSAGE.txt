Fix: Implement 3-layer alarm strategy for exact prayer time notifications

PROBLEM:
Prayer alarms were unreliable when phone is locked/sleeping:
- Alarms delayed 30-60 minutes in Doze mode
- Missing exact alarm permission on Android 12+
- Battery optimization killing background alarms
- Example: Subuh alarm at 6:30 AM triggers at 7:30 AM when phone unlocked

SOLUTION - 3-Layer Protection Strategy:

LAYER 1: AlarmManager (Primary)
- Use setAlarmClock() for highest priority (bypasses ALL restrictions)
- Fallback: setExactAndAllowWhileIdle() when permission missing
- Shows in system alarm clock apps

LAYER 2: Foreground Service (Backup)
- Starts 5 minutes before prayer time
- IMMUNE to Doze mode restrictions
- Guarantees execution at exact time
- Shows ongoing notification (keeps service alive)

LAYER 3: Heartbeat Safety Net
- Checks every 15 minutes: "Has prayer time arrived?"
- Catches missed alarms as last resort
- Maximum 15-minute delay (better than 60 min!)

EXACT ALARM PERMISSION (Critical):
- Request SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM permission on Android 12+
- Educational dialog explains importance
- Without permission: alarms become inexact (15-60 min delay)
- With permission: alarms trigger EXACTLY on time
